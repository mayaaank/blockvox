# BlockVox Demo Script (90 Seconds)

## Phase 1: The Problem & Vision (0:00 - 0:15)
- **Visual**: Start on the Home Page (`/`). Scroll through the "Why BlockVox?" section.
- **Narration**: "Traditional voting is broken—it's either private but opaque, or transparent but exposed. Welcome to BlockVox, a trustless e-voting protocol built on Avalanche that provides privacy, scalability, and absolute verifiability."

## Phase 2: Election Orchestration (0:15 - 0:35)
- **Visual**: Switch to the Admin Page (`/admin`). Toggle **Demo Mode** ON. Click "Generate Voter Tokens". Click "Start Election".
- **Narration**: "In the Admin Panel, we manage eligibility using Merkle Trees and our unique VoterPass system. With one click, we initialize a cryptographically secure election on the Fuji Testnet, ensuring only whitelisted participants can enter."

## Phase 3: The Voting Experience (0:35 - 1:00)
- **Visual**: Switch to the Vote Page (`/vote`). Toggle **Demo Mode** ON. Enter a token. Select a candidate. Hit "Commit", then "Reveal".
- **Narration**: "For the voter, it’s seamless. We use a Commit-Reveal scheme. First, your intent is hashed and locked on-chain—no one can see your vote yet. Then, you reveal it to finalize the tally. This prevents influence and double-voting while maintaining 100% privacy."

## Phase 4: Transparency & Fraud Shield (1:00 - 1:20)
- **Visual**: Switch to the Dashboard (`/dashboard`). Show the live progress bars and the AI Fraud Shield logs.
- **Narration**: "The results update instantly on our real-time Dashboard. Every tally is synced with the blockchain. Meanwhile, our AI Fraud Shield audits the audit-feed for any behavioral anomalies, ensuring every election remains fair and transparent."

## Phase 5: The Closing (1:20 - 1:30)
- **Visual**: Back to Home page or final success screen.
- **Narration**: "BlockVox isn't just a prototype—it's a vision for digital democracy. Verifiable, Private, and built for the future. Thank you."

---

### Tips for Recording:
1. **Resolution**: Record at 1080p for clarity.
2. **Audio**: Use a clear mic; ensure no background noise.
3. **Pacing**: Move your mouse intentionally and slowly so judges can follow the UI highlights.
